CBCT Interpretation
What we provide

We provide online/offisite reporting of CBCT studies. If you have a CBCT machine in your practice and need a professional report for your studies – we can help you. We provide accurate and detailed reports for your Cone Beam CT studies, self written by dental physicians who are specialized in oral and maxillofacial radiology. We provide customized reports to the dental practices according to their specific requirements.
